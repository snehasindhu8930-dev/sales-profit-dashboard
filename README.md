
# 📊 Sales & Profit Dashboard

An interactive Sales & Profit Dashboard built to analyze business performance across different dimensions such as time, category, state, and customers.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

This dashboard helps in:

- Tracking total sales and total profit
- Analyzing yearly profit trends
- Identifying top-performing product categories
- Monitoring customer contribution to profit
- Understanding regional sales performance
- Analyzing monthly sales trends

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used

- Power BI
- Excel (Data Source)
- Data Cleaning & Data Modeling
- DAX

---

## 📂 Dataset

The dataset includes:

- Order Date
- Sales
- Profit
- Customer Name
- Product Category & Sub-category
- State
- Month & Year

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Technology category generates the highest profit.
- Sales show a consistent growth from 2021 to 2024.
- Top customers contribute significantly to overall profit.
- Maximum sales are recorded in specific high-performing states.
- Monthly sales trend shows peak performance in the last quarter.

---

## 🎯 Features

- Interactive filters for:
  - Category
  - Year
  - Month
- Dynamic visuals
- Geo map for state-wise sales
- Top N customer analysis

---

## 💼 Business Use Case

This dashboard can help:

- Sales managers track performance
- Identify high-value customers
- Plan region-wise sales strategy
- Improve product category focus
